# Branded Subdomain System - Quick Start Guide

## For Specialists: Building Your Branded Page

### Step 1: Access the Page Builder
1. Log in to your specialist account
2. Click "Branded Page Builder" in the left sidebar
3. You'll see your page customization dashboard

### Step 2: Set Up Basic Info
1. Enter your **Business Name** (e.g., "Sarah's Consulting")
2. Add a **Business Tagline** (e.g., "Expert Career Coaching")
3. Set your **Page Slug** (URL-friendly name, e.g., "sarah-consulting")
   - This becomes your public URL: `https://sarah-consulting.specialistly.com`
4. Upload your **Logo URL** (link to your logo image)

### Step 3: Customize Design
1. Click the **"Design"** tab
2. Choose your **Color Scheme**:
   - **Primary Color**: Main accent color (buttons, headers)
   - **Secondary Color**: Supporting color (sections)
   - **Accent Color**: Highlight color (special elements)
3. Customize your **Header Section**:
   - Page title
   - Subtitle/tagline
   - CTA button text

### Step 4: Edit Page Sections
Click the **"Sections"** tab to customize:

- **About Section**
  - Enable/disable the section
  - Add your professional bio
  - Upload profile image

- **Services Section**
  - Enable/disable to show your services
  - Choose display mode (Grid, List, or Carousel)
  - Your active services automatically appear here

- **CTA (Call-to-Action) Section**
  - Enable/disable the section
  - Add headline and description
  - Customize button text

### Step 5: Optimize for Search (SEO)
Click the **"SEO"** tab:
- **Page Title**: What appears in Google search (50-60 characters)
- **Meta Description**: Description in search results (150-160 characters)
- **Keywords**: Search terms for your services

### Step 6: Add Social Links & Testimonials
From the main page builder:
- Add your social media links (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.)
- Add customer testimonials with ratings

### Step 7: Publish Your Page
1. Click the **"Publish"** button in top-right
2. Page status changes from "Unpublished" to "Published"
3. Your public page is now live!

### Step 8: Share Your URL
1. Click the **Copy** button next to your subdomain URL
2. Share `https://your-slug.specialistly.com` with customers
3. Customers can visit to see your branded page and book services

## For Customers: Viewing Specialist Pages

### Access a Specialist's Page
1. Type the specialist's URL in your browser:
   - Format: `https://{specialist-slug}.specialistly.com`
   - Example: `https://sarah-consulting.specialistly.com`

2. View their branded page with:
   - Business name and logo
   - About section with bio and photo
   - All their available services
   - Customer testimonials and ratings
   - Social media links
   - Call-to-action to book

### Book a Session
- Click "Book Now" on any service
- Follow the booking process
- Specialist can start your meeting directly from their dashboard

## What Gets Published

When you publish your page, customers see:
✅ Business name and branding
✅ All active services (from Services section)
✅ Your about section and bio
✅ Testimonials from customers
✅ Custom colors and design
✅ Call-to-action buttons
✅ Social media links
✅ Footer with contact info

❌ Draft pages stay private until you publish
❌ Only active services are shown
❌ Unpublished pages return 404 error

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my slug after creating it?**
A: Yes, you can edit the slug anytime in the General tab and save changes. Make sure the new slug is unique!

**Q: How long does it take to go live?**
A: Immediately after you click "Publish" button.

**Q: Can I unpublish later?**
A: Yes, click "Unpublish" button to make it private again.

**Q: What if my slug is already taken?**
A: The system will warn you. Choose a different slug. Check availability before saving.

**Q: Do my services automatically appear?**
A: Yes! All your active services from "Create / Edit Offerings" automatically display on your page.

**Q: Can customers book from my public page?**
A: Currently they see services and can click to book. Full booking integration coming soon.

**Q: Can I use my own domain?**
A: Future enhancement! Currently only subdomains are supported.

**Q: Are there view limits?**
A: No! Your page can receive unlimited views once published.

## Need Help?

- **Page not publishing?** Ensure you fill in at least business name and slug
- **Colors not showing?** Use valid hex color codes (e.g., #3B82F6)
- **Services not appearing?** Verify services are marked as "active" in Create/Edit Offerings
- **Slug errors?** Use letters, numbers, and hyphens only (no spaces or special characters)
